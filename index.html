YVES SAINT LAURENT

Y Live INTENSE, 60 ml

YSL and Adam Levine present a fresh and vibrant Y LIVE! A bright orange blossom accord and rich notes of cocoa beans are combined with woody cedar accents.

KILIAN PARIS

Perfume water Adults, 100 ml

KILIAN presents MY KIND OF LOVE - a completely new collection for young and in love: four playful fragrances, each of which challenges you and gives you the freedom to love the way you want. “I wanted to create a sure remedy for any quarrel. After all, the darlings scold - only amuse themselves. ADULTS is a very sensual scent in which the top note of fig milk captivates with the softness of naked skin, and cedar and vanilla prolong the pleasure for much longer than six minutes. DIOR

Perfume water Sauage, 100 ml

To create Sauvage Eau de Parfum, François Demachy, Dior Perfumer-Creator, drew inspiration from the desert at the magical twilight hour: mixed with the coolness of the night, the burning desert air exudes profound scents. The Sauvage Eau de Parfum is thus the olfactory interpretation of a moment tinted with a thick blue that unleashes the wildest instincts. In the hour when the wolves come out and the sky is set ablaze, a new magic unfolds. Sauvage Eau de Parfum teams the freshness of a juicy, spicy Calabrian Bergamot note with the sensuality of a mysterious and enveloping Papua New Guinean Vanilla absolute accord. The fragrance unfurls a powerful and noble trail.

KILIAN PARIS

Perfume water Rolling In Love, 50 ml

ROLLING IN LOVE is a musky scent that sits close to the skin and is dedicated to the feeling of falling in love without memory. The aroma seems to cover with a delicate veil with notes of ambrette seed absolute and almond milk accord. A sumptuous accord with seductive, sensuous middle notes of iris and freesia concrete, which make you feel ecstatic. Tonka Bean Absolute, Pure Vanilla Extract and Tuberose Absolute blend easily on the skin, accompanied by unforgettable musk notes intertwined with sweet notes, in which you can no longer distinguish between the beginning and the end.

CLIVE CHRISTIAN

1872 Masculine perfume water, 50 ml

The bottle with a heraldic motif and a golden crown cap maintains the aristocratic character of the fragrance. The composition, referring to the Roman Empire, is based on fragrant lavender and herb sage, which were considered sacred for the rulers of that time. Juicy tangerine paired with bitter orange seeds seem to quote the hot southern sun and fresh sea breeze.

ESCENTRIC MOLECULES

№2 Escentric perfume water, 100 ml

Eau de parfum belongs to the group of oriental floral unisex fragrances. The composition opens with a sweetish accord of iris and jasmine. At the heart of the perfume is the woody note of the Iso E Super molecule. Leaves a long-lasting trail of smoky ambroxan on the skin.

CREED

Perfume water Aventus by Cologne, 100 ml

Aventus by Cologne Creed is a Aromatic Fruity fragrance for men. This is a new fragrance.Aventus Cologne was launched in 2018. Perfumer: Olivier Creed. Top notes: Mandarin, Ginger and Pink pepper; middle notes: vetiver, patchouli and sandalwood; base notes: Musk, Birch, Tonka Bean and Styrax.

TOM FORD BEAUTY

Perfume water Lost Cherry, 100 ml

The intense and sophisticated oriental scent of Lost Cherry is infused with contrasts. It takes us to a place where innocence meets pleasure and sweetness meets seduction. Lost Cherry awakens unquenchable desire and excites imagination, ending with a majestic, rich sillage of sensual notes of Peruvian balsam, toasted tonka beans, sandalwood, vetiver and cedar. Basic notes: Sweet cherry, bitter almond oil, griot cherry syrup, Turkish rose, Peruvian balsam and toasted tonka beans.

MONTALE

Perfume water Vetiver des Sables, 100 ml

Unisex perfume belongs to the oriental woody group. The composition pyramid opens with light marine notes, which are set off by the piquancy of Indian spices. The base envelops with a warm, earthy trail, played by mahogany (mahogany) and vetiver. The fragrance is enclosed in a traditional aluminum cylindrical bottle.

CREED

Perfume Viking, 50 ml

Viking Creed is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for men. Viking was launched in 2017. Perfumer: Olivier Creed. Top notes: Pink pepper, Peppermint, Bergamot, Lemon, Absinthe and Orange; middle notes: Lavender, Bulgarian rose, Clove (spice), Jamaican pepper, Jasmine and Iris root; base notes: Vetiver, Cedar, White Musk and Tonka Bean.

YVES SAINT LAURENT

Y Le Parfum perfume water, 60 ml

Y Absolu opens with refreshing notes of grapefruit and apple, accentuated by the coolness of aldehydes and ginger. Geranium enhances the sparkling opening accord, while lavender and sage at the heart of the composition highlight its freshest floral facets.

GIVENCHY BEAUTY

Gentleman toilet water, 100 ml

The fragrance opens with fresh accords of sparkling bergamot, basil and cardamom, combined with a delicate and soft note of blue iris. Base notes of intense cedarwood and soft coumarin allow the scent to deepen and fully unfold on your skin. Warm contrast reveals a captivating sillage. Captivating freshness embodies the multifaceted character traits of a true Givenchy gentleman. For a confident and sensual man.
